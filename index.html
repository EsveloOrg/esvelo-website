OUR STORY

Patrick & Wojtek have been building both in tech, space and hard business industries for 30 years now. Patrick authored the first data center project on the Earth's Moon, while Wojtek created the first social network in our region with nearly 2 million users. Together they founded Esvelo to leverage the Polish potential on the global tech arena.

Esvelo's name comes from the term "escape velocity", the speed required to escape Earth's gravitational pull and venture into Space. This name embodies the spirit of the founders, aiming to help promising startups to escape their local regions and conquer global markets.
